Company Overview

NISBA is a comprehensive digital platform that democratizes Islamic finance education and provides personalized coaching for halal investing. We combine expert education, practical tools, and one-on-one coaching to help Muslims build wealth while staying true to their values.

The Problem We Solve

Key Problems
  • 1. Knowledge Gap: Complex Islamic finance principles create barriers to investment
  • 2. Trust Deficit: Muslims struggle to identify truly Shariah-compliant investments
  • 3. Lack of Guidance: No comprehensive coaching platform for halal investing exists
  • 4. Corporate Need: Companies need inclusive financial wellness programs for Muslim employees
Our Solution

Integrated Education & Coaching Platform featuring:

  • Investment Simulator: Risk-free learning with £50K virtual portfolio
  • Halal Stock Screener: AI-powered Shariah compliance checking
  • Personal Coaching: 1-on-1 sessions with certified Islamic finance experts
  • Corporate Portal: Employee financial wellness programs with DEI impact
